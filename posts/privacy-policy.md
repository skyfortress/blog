# Privacy Policy



## 1. Introduction

Welcome to our Sky Agent Shopify App. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting the personal information of both merchants and their customers. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of data we collect, how we use and protect this data, and the rights you have concerning your personal information.

We take privacy and data protection seriously, and we comply with the **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)** and other applicable data protection laws. By using our app, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and consent to the collection, use, and sharing of your information as described herein.

## 2. Information We Collect

In order to provide an optimal user experience, our AI chatbot requires access to certain data from Shopify merchants. Below is a detailed breakdown of the types of data we collect:

### 2.1 Information Collected via Shopify APIs

Our app interacts with Shopify’s APIs and requires the following permissions:

- **Products & Content:** We collect and store product and content information in a vector database to provide accurate and context-aware chatbot responses.
- **Themes:** We access theme-related data solely for the purpose of detecting whether the chatbot extension is enabled on the merchant's store.
- **Orders & Discounts:** Order and discount data are not stored permanently. Instead, this information is accessed in real-time only when requested during a customer conversation.

### 2.2 Information Provided by Merchants

We do not directly collect any personal information from merchants beyond what is provided through Shopify’s API. Our primary interaction with merchants is through the chatbot interface and the functionalities provided by Shopify.

### 2.3 Information Collected from Customers

While we do not actively collect personal information from merchants' customers, there are cases where customer-provided data may be stored:

- If a customer voluntarily provides their **email address** or **order ID** during a chatbot interaction, we may store this data temporarily to fetch relevant order details.
- Customer **conversation history** is stored within our database to improve chatbot interactions and provide a seamless conversational experience.

## 3. How We Use the Information

The data we collect is used solely to enhance the functionality of our AI chatbot. We do **not** sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Specifically, we use the collected information to:

- **Retrieve order details:** If a customer requests order-related information, we use their provided email or order ID to fetch order details via Shopify’s API.
- **Improve chatbot interactions:** Customer conversation history is stored to ensure a more personalized and efficient experience.

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

In compliance with the **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)**, we process personal data based on the following legal grounds:

- **Performance of a contract:** We process data as necessary to provide merchants with chatbot functionality.
- **Legitimate interest:** We collect and store product, content, and chat history data to enhance our chatbot’s effectiveness and ensure seamless interactions.
- **Consent:** When a customer voluntarily provides their email or order ID, we store and use this information only with their implicit consent, and solely for the purpose of retrieving order details.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain data for as long as the merchant continues to use our app. If a merchant decides to uninstall the app, all associated data is deleted from our systems. Merchants can also request data deletion at any time by contacting us directly.

## 6. Data Storage and Processing

To ensure data security and compliance with international privacy regulations, we follow strict guidelines for data storage and processing:

- All data is stored on secure servers located in **Europe**.
- Some data, such as logs and analytics, may be processed by **third-party service providers** for monitoring and troubleshooting purposes. These third parties are required to comply with strict data protection measures and do not have access to personal data for any purposes beyond system performance analysis.

## 7. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are a resident of the **European Economic Area (EEA)**, you have certain rights regarding your personal data under GDPR, including:

- **Right to Access:** You can request a copy of the data we store about you.
- **Right to Rectification:** You can request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **Right to Erasure (“Right to be Forgotten”):** You can request the deletion of your personal data.
- **Right to Restriction of Processing:** You can request that we limit the processing of your personal data under certain circumstances.
- **Right to Data Portability:** You can request to receive your data in a structured, commonly used format.
- **Right to Object:** You can object to the processing of your personal data where it is based on legitimate interests.

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, you may contact us using the details below. We will respond to your request in accordance with GDPR guidelines.

## 8. Security Measures

We take appropriate security measures to protect the integrity and confidentiality of the data we collect and store. Our security measures include, but are not limited to:

- Encryption of data at rest and in transit.
- Access controls and authentication mechanisms to prevent unauthorized access.
- Regular security audits to identify and mitigate potential risks.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time to reflect changes in our data practices, compliance requirements, or legal obligations. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your data. If we make significant changes, we will notify you by posting a revised policy with an updated **Effective Date** at the top of this page.
